Kochi Monuments Portfolio

Exploring the heritage and iconic landmarks of Kochi, Kerala

Mattancherry Palace

Also known as the Dutch Palace, this historic monument is famous for its Kerala murals and royal artifacts.

St. Francis Church

One of the oldest European churches in India, located in Fort Kochi with rich colonial history.

Paradesi Synagogue

A historic synagogue in Jew Town, showcasing the Jewish heritage of Kochi.

Bolgatty Palace

A beautiful Dutch-built palace on Bolgatty Island, now functioning as a heritage hotel.

Pallippuram Fort

One of the oldest European forts in India, located on Vypin Island near Kochi.

Hill Palace

The largest archaeological museum in Kerala and former royal residence of the Kochi rulers.
